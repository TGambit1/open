# Fun Facts Bank

Keep these short, age-appropriate, and actually delightful.

## Ages 4-6
- Octopuses have three hearts.
- A group of flamingos is called a flamboyance.

## Ages 7-9
- Honey never really spoils.
- Bananas are berries, but strawberries are not.

## Ages 10+
- Your body replaces many of its cells constantly, but not all at the same speed.
- Some trees can share nutrients through underground fungal networks.

## Rules
- avoid scary facts in morning briefings
- avoid gross facts unless specifically requested
- keep it to one fact unless asked for more
