# Seasonal Reset Checklist

## Spring
- donate too-small clothes
- check spring/summer activity gear
- clean out entryway pileups
- reset pantry and fridge basics
- replace or inspect air filters
- pick one closet or cabinet to simplify

## Summer
- prep camp or travel gear
- refresh water bottles, hats, sunscreen
- audit sandals / swimsuits / outdoor gear

## Fall
- school launch reset
- jackets, shoes, backpacks
- label supplies and activity gear

## Winter
- coat and boot check
- holiday storage plan
- medicine cabinet and pantry audit
